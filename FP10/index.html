Potential Roadblocks

  • Line Graph:
    • The labels on the line graph will be difficult to manage. As seen in the sketch of view 2, the labels can't just zoom along with hte graph, because they will be cut off. We will need to find a way to make sure that if a line is visible, its label is always visible, too.
    • Our idea of selecting supreme court justices may be better achieved by a group of buttons rather than a line graph. It's hard to say at the moment if one method will be harder, or more possible, than the other
    • Using clipping paths may prove difficult because there are so many different lines to contend with; in the mbostock example, there is only one path that is clipped
    • We have not yet seen the data that sorenson will provide for the justice swing votes, so are unsure whether or not it will create issues
  • Heatmap:
    • Creating the hover for the pie chart may be more difficult than anticipated. Just showing information in a tooltip is a backup plan, should the pie chart fail
    • Managing the transitions between so many boxes in the heatmap, and datapoints for the pie chart, may prove difficult. It might be necessary to simply create and remove an SVG every time there is an update rather than creating smoother update functions
